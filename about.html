Background

Welcome to my website! I am a current management consultant and aspiring UX Designer. I am now in my first year of the Master in Information Experience Design (IXD) program at the Pratt Institute. I have been a consultant for a little over four years and am looking to pivot to the field of UX Design where I can use my creativity more in problem-solving.

Before becoming a consultant, I majored in Operations Management and Business Analytics at the University of Maryland. My time in consulting connecting with clients has allowed me to develop skills such as stakeholder engagement, strategic communication, and adaptability. These skills will serve me well in the next chapter of my career journey.
